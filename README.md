# 🌱 Vansaarthi - Wildlife & Environmental Conservation NGO

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to Vansaarthi, where we work towards preserving nature and protecting wildlife!

🔗 **[Visit Here](https://vansaarthi.netlify.app)**

## 🎯 Our Mission
Vansaarthi is dedicated to wildlife preservation and environmental conservation. Established in 2020 with Project Rewild, we've been working tirelessly to rescue animals, plant trees, and create a sustainable future.

## ✨ What We Do
- 🦁 Wildlife Rescue & Rehabilitation
- 🌳 Tree Plantation & Maintenance
- 🌿 Environmental Conservation
- 👥 Community Empowerment

## 🚀 Our Impact
- 5000+ Animals Rescued
- 10000+ Trees Planted
- 50+ Communities Served
- 1000+ Active Volunteers

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Authentication: JWT, Google OAuth
